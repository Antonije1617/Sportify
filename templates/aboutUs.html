About Sportify :)

Our goal is to bring together students of FH Campus Wien who are interested in sporting activities. To join the community just register with your @stud.fh-campuswien.ac.at address, afterwards you can book courses or offer some. The registration is free and eventual course costs are just to cover costs. Don´t waste time, register and start socializing and get fit with your new friends :)
